About

Is it worth it?

If you have asked yourself this question when considering a coding boot camp, you are not alone.

As coding boot camp students, we understand that researching information about all the programs out there can be a daunting task. We've compiled a database with information provided to us from graduates from several coding boot camps in Chicago. We hope this information will make the search a little bit easier for potential students and provide some insight on the possible return on your investment. Some of the data we have collected is:

  • Overall Rating of Boot Camp Experience
    Based a scale of 1 to 5. The factors we've taken into considertion are: Curriculum, Instructors, and Job Assistance.
  • Job Found
    Graduates were asked whether or not they found a job upon graduation from their boot camp.
  • Job Position
    The job positions in which graduates found employment post graduation.
  • Salary Range
    The range of salaries earned by graduates in their new job positions.
